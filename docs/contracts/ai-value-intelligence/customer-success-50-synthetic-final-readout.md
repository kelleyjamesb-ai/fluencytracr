Status: synthetic client-facing rehearsal

Phase: `phase-ai-value-synthetic-cs-50-pilot-data`

## Executive Summary

This synthetic Customer Success pilot shows what a final, client-readable
FluencyTracr and AI Value readout could look like after a 50-person Customer
Success function completes the 24-item AI Fluency instrument and supplies
aggregate Customer Success operating metrics for the same pilot motion.

The synthetic result supports a governed, caveated value discussion for
`customer_success_account_health_review`. It does not prove ROI, causality,
productivity, headcount impact, person-level assessment, comparative manager
or group assessment, or customer-facing financial value.

Aggregate AI work evidence is present: Search, Assistant, Skill, and governed
agent activity increased in the comparison window, while verification increased
and recovery/abandonment decreased. VBD movement shows a broader, more
repeatable AI-supported account health review posture, not causality or ROI
proof.

## Value Interpretation

Allowed client-safe statement:

> Aggregate workflow metrics and AI work evidence can support internal planning
> for a risk reduction investigation.

What the synthetic final can say:

- Account health review work shows directionally better aggregate movement
  across retention-risk, coverage, timeliness, and follow-up indicators.
- AI work evidence shows higher aggregate Search, Assistant, Skill, and agent
  activity in the comparison window, with stronger verification and lower
  friction.
- VBD movement shows the AI operating pattern becoming faster, broader, and
  more embedded in repeatable account health review work.
- AI Fluency movement shows the function aggregate becoming more confident,
  higher quality, and more behaviorally ready to use AI in real Customer Success
  workflows.
- The combined posture is strong enough for executive validation of a
  caveated value investigation.

What it cannot say:

- Glean or FluencyTracr caused the outcome movement.
- The pilot produced realized ROI.
- The pilot produced dollarized customer-facing economic value.
- Individuals, managers, or groups improved or underperformed.
- Lower QBR prep time proves productivity.
- Retention movement can be attributed to AI without customer-owned validation.
